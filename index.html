Hi, I'm Haley!

Clinical Research Coordinator @ Autism Assessment, Research, Treatment, and Services Center (AARTS), Rush University Medical Center

Purdue University Alumna, B.S. Psychological Sciences & B.A. Spanish

I am a researcher focused on translational, intervention-focused research for autism, developmental disabilities, and neurogenetic populations, with experience in clinical trials, independent research, and mixed-methods approaches.

I have disseminated my research at national and international conferences and contribute to ongoing manuscripts.

My clinical experience implementing evidence-based interventions, including PEERS and the Summer Treatment Program for autistic adolescents, informs my goal of conducting intervention-focused research that can improve adaptive functioning and social outcomes.

Interests: Autism, developmental disabilities, and neurogenetic syndromes; intervention research and implementation, parent-implemented interventions; adaptive functioning and challenging behaviors.

Experience

Currently a Clinical Research Coordinator for a behavioral RCT

Lead coordinator for a behavioral clinical trial RCT, (WINGS), examining the efficacy and implementation of a caregiver-implemented, telehealth-based behavioral intervention tailored for children with genetic syndromes and co-occurring intellectual disability.

Prior research experiences across autism, developmental disability, & community-focused research

Research experience spanning autism, developmental disabilities, and community-based studies, with work both in quantitative and qualitative data collection and analysis.

Clinical experience with children with autism and developmental disabilities

Experience implementing evidence-based social skills and behavioral interventions, such as PEERS and the Summer Treatment Program, with adolescents and children with autism and developmental disabilities.
